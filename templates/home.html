Medicare Hospital Information by State

Andrew McConnell

The purpose of this data visualization suite is to provide an interactive comparison of healthcare information across US territories. The information contained was provided by the official datasets used on the Medicare.gov Hospital Compare Website, and can be downloaded here.

This suite was generated using a combination of Python 2.7 (particularly bokeh 0.11.1 for the charts and plots), Django and HTML. Plots are generated upon request rather than stored. Some datasets may be incomplete. Below you may select the dataset and the specific state to analyze.
